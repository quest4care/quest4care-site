Why Henry & Madison County

The Data Chose This Community.

QCLS was founded here because the need is documented, the gaps are measurable, and the community deserves an accountable system designed to close them.

17.3% Madison County

of residents under 65 live with a disability — above state average, requiring coordinated access to services that too often remain fragmented.

16.6% Henry County

of residents under 65 live with a disability. More than 22% of Henry County residents rely on Medicaid-supported healthcare coverage.

40% Indiana ALICE Threshold

of Indiana households fall below the combined federal poverty and ALICE survival threshold — one emergency away from serious instability.

30–50 Miles to Specialized Care

Many residents must routinely travel 30–50 miles for specialized healthcare, behavioral health, disability, and social support services.

The National Context

More than 60% of federally designated Mental Health Professional Shortage Areas are located within rural communities — communities exactly like ours. The nonprofit organizations serving these communities face growing pressure to demonstrate measurable outcomes while operating with limited infrastructure, aging technology systems, and minimal administrative capacity.

Nationally, approximately 30% of nonprofit organizations fail within 10 years, and more than 50% operate with less than one month of financial reserves. QCLS was built to address these structural failures at the community level — not by adding another isolated service, but by building the accountability infrastructure that makes every existing service work better.

The data did not simply suggest Henry and Madison County. It confirmed what 20+ years of direct operational leadership in Medicaid-funded disability services, nonprofit administration, and community support operations made undeniable — this community needs a catalyst.

For Nonprofits

Build the Foundation
Funders Require.

FoundationReady™ is a structured capacity assessment built on the CARES Framework. We evaluate your organization across five domains and deliver a roadmap for sustainable funding and governance excellence.

The FoundationReady™ Assessment

Every assessment delivers scored results with written justification tied to specific evidence — not generic observations. Each finding is clearly labeled Evidence, Assessment, or Recommended so your team knows exactly where you stand and why.

The result is a clear, honest picture of your organization's infrastructure maturity and a prioritized roadmap for improvement — the foundation funders are increasingly required to see.

For Individuals & Families

Help That Follows
Through to Access.

QCLS is not a 211 list. We are human navigators who make contact, confirm services, follow up, and document access — because a referral is only complete when someone actually gets help.

01

Intake & Needs Identification

We listen to understand your full situation — not just the presenting need. Every person has a unique set of circumstances and goals.

02

Verified Referral Coordination

We connect you to services we know are active, accepting, and appropriate — not a list of phone numbers to call on your own.

03

Follow-Up & Barrier Removal

We follow up to confirm you received the service. If something blocked access, we work to remove that barrier before closing the case.

04

Access Confirmed & Documented

Cases are only closed when access is confirmed — not when a referral was made. This is the QCLS difference.

About QCLS

Built on Nearly
Two Decades of
Systems Experience.

QuestCare LifeStyle Solutions was founded by MarQuest Higgins, who brings nearly two decades of operational leadership in Medicaid-funded disability services — including compliance, financial controls, board governance, program documentation, and outcome measurement from the inside.

QCLS received its 501(c)(3) determination on January 24, 2025. We are incorporated in Indiana, operating with a documented strategic plan, an independent governing board, and a clear three-pillar mission.

We are not consultants. We are a community systems organization — building the accountability infrastructure that enables nonprofits to earn trust, access funding, and deliver measurable outcomes.
